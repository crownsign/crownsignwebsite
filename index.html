Crown Sign Systems (CSS) is an innovative

supplier of retail signage and graphic displays. As we guide your project from design through installation, we differentiate ourselves from the competition through quality products and unparalleled customer service.

Crown Sign Systems, a WBE, offers a quality range of interior and exterior signage, from design services, fabrication & installation in our state of the art manufacturing facility. We are comprised of hard working professionals including a team of dedicated project managers; we are committed to getting your project done from concept to installation.

Services

Project Management

With a staff of some 70 experienced professionals, Crown Sign Systems is able to assign a dedicated project manager to each job who will provide personalized customer service and our hallmark attention to detail.

Survey & Suggestion

We will inspect the area where work is proposed to determine what size sign best fits the site and our customers’ goals. Our gifted estimators will provide an accurate price for your budget. Be prepared for your estimator to ask you not just many questions about the job, but the right questions. This estimation and bid process leads to fewer change orders down the road, because our estimators already are very familiar with the job.

Location Plans & Schedules

If you are not sure of what signs you need and where, we can provide a location plan and corresponding message schedule for easy review helping to confirm accurate quantities and code compliancy.

Design Consultation

Whether you have visual references, or you are starting with a blank slate, we can help take your idea conception to completion with our design build or full design package services.

Code Recommendations

Although we are not code consultants, with over 22 years of experience we have picked up a thing or two. We strive to follow standard ANSI ADA codes and will can help adjust existing designs to meet building code requirements.

Manufacturing

With our 15,000 sq feet of manufacturing space we have the benefit of manufacturing a large amount of work in house, keeping cost and turnaround time to a minimum. Our knowledgeable production staff can assist in value engineering any design to meet your budget.

Installation

Whether it is Non-Union, or Union install, our fleet of experienced installers give us the flexibility to meet your schedule and deadline. Our respectful and courteous crew will give you piece of mind that there will be no public disturbances during an installation.

Certified WBE

Crown Sign is a certified Women Business Enterprise (WBE). We also are certified by New York State’s School Construction Authority, Empire State Development, Port Authority of NY & NJ and the Dormitory Authority of the State of New York.

Quality products and customer satisfaction

There is no project too big or too small. We are dedicated to seeing your project from concept to completion.
