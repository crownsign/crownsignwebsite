Dream Downtown

Designing the interior of the Dream Downtown Hotel, the architects describe how they aimed to evoke “an otherworldly feel.” Crown Sign mirrored this style as it created code compliant signs for the otherworldly interiors. We drew inspiration from the porthole motif found on Dream Downtown’s interior and exterior.
